ProfessorTextbooks

Change the way you think about textbooks.

A Education Revolution.

ProfessorTextbooks is a unique website that allows you to not only find your textbooks, but also find out whether or not you ACTUALLY need them. We'll tell you up front, no bias, no gimmicks, just pure fact.

Reviews That Work For You

Love us? Send us an email. Hate us? Send us an email. Want a book? Write a request. Have a book? Write a review. We are students looking for ways to better our service, and who else should we ask than the very people who use it.

Purely Peers.

ProfessorTextbooks is run by students for students. At the University of Michigan, we look out for our fellow Wolverines, whether it's at the Big House or the UGLi. It's about time we start to use that mindset for buying our textbooks too.

Michigan, and beyond?

Our vision is to see ProfessorTextbooks spread across the country. Textbook prices are a highway robbery, and we need to save all the money we can for Pizza House and Ricks. But it all starts here. With your help, we can help kids across the country save thousands of dollars and start to help kids focus back on the education, not the price.
