You Review. We review. EVERYONE saves..

As college students, we began to feel a pain. The pain was our wallets burning a hole in our
pockets as we spent hundreds and thousands on textbooks. As we did this, nothing frustrated us more than when we bought a textbook, and ended up never using it.
So we set out to save ourselves some money, and other people money as well. By creating a community of peers all providing their input on textbooks and their true value,
we can revolutionize the education system.
